What Faction are you aligned with?



Question #1: What do you like to do in your free time?

The Heroes Army


The strongest military in the Multiverse, you desire to protect people and help those who cannot do so themselves. Sometimes, you persist through the most difficult of choices.


You cannot stand when others get hurt, choosing to put yourself between others and harm to protect those you care about the most.

The Rebels


The largest anarchist group in the Multiverse, you desire to unite everyone under the same goal, yet you secretly also crave some semblance of spreading influence to others.


You cannot stand when inequality prevails, choosing to implement justice your own way without paying heed to any moral obligations or weakness.

The Federation Army


The strongest confederate powerhouse in the Multiverse, you desire to work towards your goals with like-minded people and gain as much influence as possible over other people's way of thinking.


You cannot stand when people act privileged and haughty, desiring to put them in their place and show people who really is in control.

The Imperial Legion


The strongest dictatorial force in the Multiverse, you desire absolute power and control over those who do not abide by the rules nor have respect for the process of things.


You cannot stand when people are ignorant of other people like yourself, choosing to solve problems with absolute authority.
